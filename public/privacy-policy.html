Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

Who We Are

This website is operated by Al-Hakeem's Enterprises LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), doing business as Al-Hakeem's Tonsorial. We are a barbershop located in Owings Mills, Maryland. Our website address is alhakeems.com.

What Data We Collect and Why

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve the site and better serve our clients. Google Analytics collects the following types of information automatically:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search engine
  • General geographic location (city-level, not your exact address)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Session duration and interaction events (such as clicks)

This data is aggregated and anonymous. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information through Google Analytics.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses first-party cookies (small text files stored on your device) to distinguish unique visitors and track sessions. The cookies used include:

  • _ga — Used to distinguish unique users. Expires after 2 years.
  • _ga_[ID] — Used to maintain session state. Expires after 2 years.

These cookies do not contain personal information. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on other websites.

How Your Data Is Used

The analytics data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand which pages are most useful to visitors
  • Identify how visitors find our website
  • Improve the content and usability of our site

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics is provided by Google LLC. Google may process data on servers located in the United States. Google's use of the collected data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. IP addresses are anonymized by default in Google Analytics 4.

Your Choices

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by:

  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Disabling cookies in your browser settings
  • Using a browser with built-in tracking protection

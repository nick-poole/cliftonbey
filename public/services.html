Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

All appointments are booked through theCut app. Visit my booking page to view available services, select your preferred time, and reserve your appointment. Walk-ins are not guaranteed, so booking in advance is recommended.

What is your cancellation policy?

I ask that clients cancel or reschedule their appointment through theCut app with reasonable notice. Late cancellations and no-shows may affect your ability to book priority time slots in the future. Respecting your barber's time ensures the best experience for all clients.

What is your refund policy?

Services are non-refundable once completed. Membership dues are collected on the first of each month and renew automatically. Unused haircuts do not roll over. If you have a concern about your service, contact us directly so we can make it right.

Where is Al-Hakeem's Tonsorial located?

Al-Hakeem's Tonsorial is located inside Brookside Barbershop:

9419 Common Brook Rd
Owings Mills, MD 21117

I serve clients from Owings Mills, Baltimore, and throughout Baltimore County.

Do I need an appointment, or do you accept walk-ins?

Appointments are strongly recommended and can be booked through theCut app. Walk-ins are accommodated when availability allows, but booking ahead ensures you get the time slot that works best for your schedule.

What should I expect during my first visit?

First-time clients should book the New Client / Reset Cut, which includes a full consultation. During this visit, Clifton will assess your hair texture, face shape, and grooming goals to create a customized cut. Expect a professional, comfortable experience tailored specifically to you.

About Clifton Bey

For over 30 years, Clifton Bey has embodied the art of barbering. As the founder of Al-Hakeem’s Tonsorial, Clifton brings a unique blend of skill, tradition, and modern technique to every haircut, beard trim, and grooming service. Known for his precision and dedication, Clifton has earned a reputation as a master barber who transforms every visit into a personalized experience.

Clifton's journey began in Brooklyn, NY, where he developed a passion for the barbering craft in the vibrant neighborhoods of Coney Island, Bed-Stuy, and Brownsville. Over time, his skills evolved into a distinct style that balances traditional techniques with contemporary trends. After moving to Maryland in 1996, Clifton continued to hone his expertise, earning the respect of clients and colleagues alike.

Today, Al-Hakeem’s Tonsorial represents more than a barbershop—it’s a testament to Clifton’s commitment to excellence. The name "Al-Hakeem" reflects wisdom and precision, qualities that guide Clifton's approach to his work and his clients. Whether you’re seeking a classic cut, a sharp fade, or expert beard sculpting, Clifton delivers grooming services designed to elevate your look and confidence.

  • 30+ years of barbering experience
  • Expert in classic cuts, modern fades, and beard sculpting
  • Personalized grooming tailored to your style
  • A trusted name in Maryland's barbering community

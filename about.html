About Clifton

For over three decades, Clifton "Al-Hakeem" Bey has been more than a barber—he is a curator of style, a craftsman of confidence, and a keeper of tradition. As the mastermind behind Al-Hakeem’s Tonsorial, Clifton has seamlessly blended the artistry of classic barbering with the innovation of modern grooming. His chair is not just a seat but a sanctuary, where every cut, shave, and style is an ode to precision, individuality, and timeless sophistication.

Clifton, a New York City native, carries the city’s energy and resilience into every aspect of his craft. His barbering journey began in Maryland in 1996, where he started his apprenticeship, laying the foundation for a career marked by dedication and mastery. By 1998, he had earned his journeyman’s certification, and in 1999, he achieved his master barber’s license—a testament to his commitment to excellence.

Over the years, Clifton has built a reputation as a trusted and skilled barber in the community. His expertise extends beyond sharp fades and precise beard sculpting; it’s rooted in a belief that barbering is a transformative experience. For Clifton, every client represents a unique opportunity to combine artistry and self-expression, creating looks that inspire confidence and authenticity.

Al-Hakeem’s Tonsorial is a reflection of its namesake. The word “Al-Hakeem,” meaning “the wise” and “the judicious,” captures the essence of Clifton’s approach: thoughtful, deliberate, and refined. It’s a name that carries weight, much like his work. Whether delivering a timeless cut, crafting a contemporary fade, or perfecting a beard trim, Clifton’s precision and artistry are unmatched. Each detail is a testament to his decades of experience, and each client leaves not just groomed but transformed.

Today, Clifton continues to shape more than hair—he shapes stories, legacies, and identities. Al-Hakeem’s Tonsorial is more than a service; it’s a statement. It’s where tradition meets innovation, where the past informs the future, and where every client finds their unique edge. Clifton Bey doesn’t just cut hair; he elevates it to an experience—a craft rooted in wisdom, executed with passion, and celebrated in style.

With one chair, one vision, and unmatched skill, Clifton "Al-Hakeem" Bey turns every session into a masterpiece. Precision, artistry, and dedication define his craft, ensuring every client leaves the chair with confidence and style. In his chair, a haircut transforms into a bold expression of self.
